NOTE: replicaCount defaults to 1 and autoscaling is disabled. The realtime WebSocket chat +
huddle layer keeps its connection registry and huddle state IN-PROCESS (no Redis/broker
fan-out) — running more than one replica silently breaks cross-replica message/huddle
delivery. Do not scale this Deployment out without first adding a shared broker (ADR-0010
§Honest deferrals).

NOTE: Vault/KMS and mTLS are NOT provisioned by this chart — Rendly's own secret (the ES256
JWT signing key) is delivered as a native K8s Secret (ADR-0010 §Honest deferrals, mirroring
Anoryx-AI-Orchestrator's own O-008 deferral of the same).
